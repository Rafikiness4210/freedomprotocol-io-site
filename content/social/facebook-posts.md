### Facebook 4 — Passphrase Explainer

Question for everyone with a hardware wallet:

Are you using a passphrase?

Not your PIN. Not your seed phrase. A passphrase — an additional word or phrase that generates an entirely separate hidden wallet.

If someone finds your seed phrase, they get a decoy wallet. Your real holdings are invisible. There's no indicator it even exists.

This is the most underused security feature in crypto. Setup takes 10 minutes on Ledger or Trezor.

Step-by-step guide: freedomprotocol.io/blog/passphrase-vs-seed-phrase-security
